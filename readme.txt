# 🐆 Léopardo AI

**Léopardo AI** is an intelligent content management and marketing automation platform built for digital marketers, freelancers, and growing businesses.

📦 Available on **Windows**, **Linux**, **macOS**, and **Mobile**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- 📅 **Plan & Automate Posts**  
  Schedule recurring posts on Facebook Groups, LinkedIn, and more.

- 🖼️ **Organize Marketing Media**  
  Categorize and store images, videos, and files used in your campaigns.

- 📝 **Save & Reuse Descriptions**  
  Store frequently-used text for easy access during campaigns and prospecting.

- 🌐 **Assisted Group Publishing**  
  Open 10+ groups in your browser and quickly paste content & media. Perfect for repetitive daily posting.

- 🔗 **Save Useful Business Links**  
  Save websites you frequently use for work, categorized by purpose.

- 🧾 **Prospect Management Module**  
  Keep track of potential clients before moving them to your CRM.

- 🔒 **Privacy First**  
  All your marketing strategies and data are stored **locally** — no cloud, no risks.

- 🧩 **Browser Extensions**  
  Extensions available for Chrome and Firefox for assisted publishing.

- 🌍 **Multilingual & Cross-platform**  
  Available in multiple languages and platforms.
